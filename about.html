Our Story

Rooted in Nature.
Crafted for You.

AL-Jabrisafi was born from a powerful belief — that Africa's botanical heritage holds some of the world's most effective and beautiful skincare secrets. For generations, African communities have turned to the land for healing, nourishment and beauty. We honour that heritage.

Our name speaks to our purpose: Al-Jabri means "the one who heals and repairs" — and Safi means "pure." Together, they capture everything we stand for.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Purity First

Every formula begins and ends with one question: is it pure? We never compromise on ingredients. No sulphates, parabens, artificial fragrance or harmful chemicals — ever.

African Heritage

We celebrate and preserve the rich botanical wisdom of African beauty traditions. Every ingredient we source tells a story of the land, the people and the generations who came before.

Compassion

We are proudly cruelty-free and vegan. We believe beauty should never come at the cost of suffering — to animals, to people, or to the planet.

Sustainability

We source ingredients responsibly and work toward eco-conscious packaging. Caring for the earth is not just a value — it's a commitment we renew with every product we make.

Premium Quality

We produce in small batches to ensure every product meets our exacting standards. From the sourcing of raw materials to the final seal on the bottle — quality is non-negotiable.

Community

We are for our community — the women and men of Kenya who deserve access to premium natural beauty. We celebrate your skin, your hair, your heritage.

The Journey

How It All Began

AL-Jabrisafi started with a simple frustration — the inability to find genuinely pure, effective, and affordable natural beauty products that truly honoured African skin and hair.

We went back to the source. To the herbs, oils, and botanical extracts that African grandmothers used for centuries. We partnered with local farmers and ethical sourcing cooperatives across Kenya to bring these powerful ingredients to life in modern formulations.

Today, every product we make is a bridge between the wisdom of the past and the science of the present — honouring heritage while delivering results that modern skin and hair demands.

"We don't just sell products. We carry forward a legacy of African beauty, healing and self-care."
